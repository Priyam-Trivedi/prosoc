About Us

PROSOC is a social impact startup incubated at IIT Kanpur and IIM Bangalore. As a purpose-driven enterprise, we are committed to designing innovative, scalable, and sustainable solutions that uplift underserved communities, particularly in the areas of education and health.

  • Aims to reach 100 million children by 2026.
  • Used by 8,50,000+ students across 22 Indian states
  • DESKIT boosts learning by 15–20% and increases attendance by 5–10%.
  • Supports livelihood through local manufacturing
  • Impact in refugee camps (Iraq & Kurdistan)
  • Improves posture, concentration, handwriting, and school attendance.
  • Alternative to heavy and fixed furniture in space-constrained schools
  • It supports better posture and encourages student motivation.

We are on a mission to empower 100 million + students across India by providing access to basic study infrastructure. Our flagship product,DESKIT is a transformational educational tool designed to solve a infrastructure problem faced by millions of underprivileged children.DESKIT is a multi-functional school bag that transforms into a personal study table — portable, lightweight, and child-friendly. It is aimed at enhancing the learning environment of children who lack access to basic study infrastructure both at school and at home. Whether it's a classroom with no furniture or a cramped home environment, DESKIT allows any space to become a productive study area. The product is based on the philosophy of: "Sit Right, Set Write" — encouraging proper posture, focused attention, and a better overall learning experience.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is DESKIT?

DESKIT is a unique combination of a lightweight school bag and an ergonomic,foldable, lightweight, portable, and detachable study table.

What is the impact of DESKIT?

● Students will have better sitting posture and viewing angle while studying. ● The learning level among students can increase by 15-20% as they will be able to concentrate better when they are able to sit properly. ● With a proper study table to use, children’s handwriting will improve. ● DESKIT will act as a child’s personal learning space both at school and at home, instilling a sense of ownership in them. ● Students will be motivated to attend school and study. Attendance level can increase by 5-10%

What are DESKIT features?

The table can be adjusted at two heights to maintain a comfortable sitting posture. ● The detached table can also be used separately from the bag. ● The school bag also has compartments for keeping books and a lunch box. Besides, it has a water bottle pouch on the side and an extra pouch for keeping stationery. ● DESKIT is easier to carry and can be used both at school and home.

Which all Size are available?

DESKIT is available in two sizes: The 14L model is ideal for students in classes 1 to 5 (28×13×39 cm, 1.25 kg), and the 25L model suits classes 6 to 10 (31.7×17×44 cm, 1.30 kg). Both feature an adjustable table with two height options (8" and 11") and are available in Black-Red and Black-Blue. Note: Colors can be customized for bulk orders of 2,000+ unit

Is Prosoc Innovators a nonprofit/ NGO?

No. PROSOC Innovators Pvt.Ltd. is a for-profit social enterprise.

Can Prosoc Innovators directly implement a CSR project?

No. Being a private limited company, Prosoc can work with Foundations which can directly procure DESKITs from Prosoc Innovators and provide them to the children in their community. Prosoc also has a network of NGO partners who can be introduced into the project for on ground implementation.
